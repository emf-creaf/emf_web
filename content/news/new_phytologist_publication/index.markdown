Although process-based models are an attractive tool for monitoring live fuel
moisture content (LFMC) dynamics, their application to operationally assess
near-term wildfire danger at regional scale faces important challenges. We
attached these sources of uncertainty using the MEDFATE modeling framework in
our last study recently published in New Phytologist:

Balaguer-Romano, R., Sañé, A., Martin-StPaul, N., Ruffault, J., Gabriel, E.,
Castro, X., Pimont, F., Liu, X., Druel, A., Delzon, S. and De Cáceres, M.
**(2026)**, Key sources of uncertainty in process-based modeling of live fuel
moisture content. *New Phytol*.
[https://doi.org/10.1111/nph.71286](https://doi.org/10.1111/nph.71286)
